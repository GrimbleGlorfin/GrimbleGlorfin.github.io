Holson Proudfoot

Backstory

He lived in his family home in Senshara for a while, dabling slightly in dark magic. One day he left in search of adventure and stumbled upon Ort stuck in a hole. Later he met Hollow, who pledged himself as Holson's vassal and led them into the Underdark. There they met Ralof and the princess Hedge.

Details

Part of the Call of Cthulhu campaign along with Ort and Ralof. After journeying together for a while, they get separated when Ort and Ralof get carried off by a dragon. Holson soon after meets the Old Man who instructs him in the art of dark magic. He also reveals a way for Holson to attain lichdom. Later, Holson manages to collect what he needs for the ritual and becomes a Lich, but he also is able to retain most of his humanity. Holson, along with Ort and Ralof also defeat Cthulhu, banishing him back to the Abyssal Plain. Holson later reappears in Three Cards Left when he comes to a council at Daleth along with other memorable characters from Call of Cthulhu. Later on he helps Ort comprise the epic compilation of memoirs called The Celestrianth.
