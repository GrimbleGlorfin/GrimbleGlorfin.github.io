Astarius

Description

A world built on the body of the First Dragon and the ashes of the Phoenix. Astarius is located directly above the Elemental Plain in the reflection of material and nonmaterial worlds. Its gate and sphere float in the farthest reaches of the Abyssal Plain. It was one of the few worlds completely untouched by Corvan during The Radiant War, however, this led way for The Watcher to rule it for many millenia. Though the world of Astarius was technically created during the Radiant War, its location in the Abyssal Plain and the magic that created it, caused the world to age starting at the beginning of the timeline (all the worlds had aligned at the present time by the beginning of The Golden Age). Astarius is one of the oldest non-corpse worlds due to this fact. Many of its withered siblings make up the Shadowfell.
